Easy Lasagna

This lasagna recipe is a delicious and hearty dish that combines layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's perfect for a comforting family meal or a special occasion. The recipe is easy to follow and yields a flavorful lasagna that everyone will love.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer the sauce for 15 minutes.
  7. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and béchamel in a baking dish.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
